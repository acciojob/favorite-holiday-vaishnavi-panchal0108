spiti valley


Spiti Valley is a beautiful cold desert valley located in the Himalayan region of Himachal Pradesh, India. Known for its breathtaking mountains, ancient Buddhist monasteries, and adventurous roads, it is one of the most popular travel destinations in North India. The valley is situated at a high altitude and is surrounded by snow-capped peaks and barren landscapes. Major attractions include Key Monastery, Chandratal Lake, Kaza, Tabo, and Hikkim village.
